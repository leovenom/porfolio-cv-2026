Automating the email production pipeline with AI

Designed an end-to-end AI pipeline for lifecycle email production. Claude generates the content from the brief; Cursor assembles and ships the responsive templates (HTML/CSS + Liquid); Braze executes the campaign. The handoff between steps is structured JSON, which makes the whole flow versionable and reviewable instead of hand-authored every time. The same pipeline feeds the A/B variants — subject-line and CTA tests running at 3M+ sends per arm, analysed in Hex.

<15 min per campaign — down from 3–8 hours
